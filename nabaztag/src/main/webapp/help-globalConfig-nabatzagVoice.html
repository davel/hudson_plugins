Provide the name of the voice you want your rabbit use to announce the build result.

Samples:

  • FR-Maxence
  • UK-Mistermuggles
  • US-Billye
  • DE-Otto
  • ...
You can send a request to your bunny to discover all supported voices. Use http://api.nabaztag.com/vl/FR/api.jsp?sn=YOUR-SERIAL&token=YOUR-TOKEN&action=9 to find it for your bunny (requires serial number and token).
